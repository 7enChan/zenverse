概述

鱼忘签团队致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解我们如何收集、使用、存储、处理和保护您的个人信息。请在使用鱼忘签应用之前仔细阅读本隐私政策。

我们收集的信息

鱼忘签是一款极简风格的待办事项管理应用，我们只收集完成核心功能所必需的最少数据：

  1. 用户创建的内容：您创建的任务、和相关设置。
  2. 设备信息：设备类型、操作系统版本和应用版本号，用于故障排除和兼容性保障。

重要说明：以上信息都只存储在您的设备本地，这些信息不会被传输或存储在我们的服务器上。

数据存储与传输

  1. 本地存储：您创建的所有任务和设置默认仅存储在您的设备上，不会传输到我们的服务器。
  2. iCloud 同步：如果您启用 iCloud 同步功能，您的任务数据将通过 Apple 的 CloudKit 服务在您的多个设备间同步。这些数据存储在您的个人 iCloud 账户中，受 Apple 隐私政策保护。我们无法访问您同步到 iCloud 的数据。

数据保留与删除

  1. 本地数据：存储在设备上的数据将保留到您删除相应任务或卸载应用为止。
  2. iCloud数据：通过 iCloud 同步的数据将保留在您的 iCloud 账户中，直到您删除相应数据或按照Apple政策被自动清理。

如需删除所有数据，您可以：

  • 在应用内删除所有任务和设置
  • 卸载应用并通过 iCloud 设置删除相关备份数据

儿童隐私

我们的服务面向所有年龄段的用户。我们不会有意收集 13 岁以下儿童的个人信息。

隐私政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。当我们进行重大变更时，将在应用内通知您并提供更新后的隐私政策链接。我们鼓励您定期查看本政策，以了解我们如何保护您的信息。

联系我们

如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：zen@宙.space

Overview

The Finlet team is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy aims to help you understand how we collect, use, store, process, and protect your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using the Finlet application.

Information We Collect

Finlet is a minimalist to-do list management application. We only collect the minimum data necessary to deliver core functionality:

  1. User-created content: Tasks you create and related settings.
  2. Device information: Device type, operating system version, and application version number for troubleshooting and compatibility purposes.

Important note: All of the above information is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to or stored on our servers.

Data Storage and Transfer

  1. Local storage: All tasks and settings you create are stored only on your device by default and are not transmitted to our servers.
  2. iCloud synchronization: If you enable iCloud sync, your task data will be synchronized across your multiple devices through Apple's CloudKit service. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is protected by Apple's Privacy Policy. We cannot access your data synchronized to iCloud.

Data Retention and Deletion

  1. Local data: Data stored on your device will be retained until you delete the corresponding tasks or uninstall the application.
  2. iCloud data: Data synchronized through iCloud will remain in your iCloud account until you delete the corresponding data or it is automatically cleared according to Apple's policies.

To delete all data, you can:

  • Delete all tasks and settings within the application
  • Uninstall the application and delete related backup data through iCloud settings

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you within the application and provide a link to the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to understand how we protect your information.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: zen@宙.space
